Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

AcademicPro protects personal data, communication, and order confidentiality. This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Account details: name, email, phone, profile photo, and preferences.
  • Order details: project briefs, academic level, deadlines, and uploaded files.
  • Communication logs between client, writer, and admin for support and dispute handling.
  • Payment records and transaction status (processed by payment providers).

How We Use Data

  • Process orders, assign writers, and deliver work.
  • Facilitate communication between clients and writers.
  • Handle payouts, refunds, and reconciliation.
  • Protect platform security and prevent abuse.
  • Improve service quality and policy compliance.

Third-Party Sharing

We only share data with trusted service providers needed to operate the platform. Where configured, this includes payment processors and analytics providers. These providers process data under their own privacy policies and security standards.

Data Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards such as encrypted connections, access controls, and account security features to protect your data. Sensitive payment information is handled by payment processors.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to receive a copy of your data. For California residents, you also have the right to know, delete, and opt out of certain data sharing.

Data Retention

We keep personal data for the duration your account is active and for up to three years afterward to handle disputes, compliance, and fraud prevention, unless a longer period is required by law.

Confidentiality

Files and messages are limited to authorized parties (client, assigned writer, admin). Access controls are enforced per order.

Contact Us

If you have privacy questions or requests, contact support@academicpro.com.
