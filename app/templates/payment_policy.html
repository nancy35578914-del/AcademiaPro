Payment, Refund, and Revision Policy

Transparent terms designed to protect clients and ensure fair delivery standards.

Payment Process

  • Client deposits are recorded at order submission.
  • Orders are reviewed by admin before writer assignment.
  • Final release follows client approval or dispute resolution.

Revisions

  • Revisions are accepted when aligned with original instructions.
  • Major scope changes may require re-quotation.
  • Revision timelines are tracked in the order communication thread.

Refunds

  • Refund requests are reviewed by admin with order evidence.
  • Possible outcomes: full refund, partial refund, or continued revision.
  • Support tickets are the official channel for refund disputes.

Integrity

All work follows originality standards. Similarity report summaries are included with delivery.
